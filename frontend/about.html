About Happy Paws

Happy Paws is a passionate community-based organization in Ambalangoda, Sri Lanka, dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and stray dogs. Founded on the belief that every dog deserves love, care, and a family, we work tirelessly to give second chances to dogs in need.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to rescue dogs from the streets, provide them with proper medical care, nutrition, training, and most importantly, unconditional love ‚Äì and then help them find their forever homes with caring families.
